Hi, I am Soraya Carvajal

Communications Professional and Frontend Developer, based in Spain

I am a Communications professional with extensive training and experience in Colombia and Spain. My background ranges from different positions in corporate communications, where I have designed and implemented strategic, internal and external communication plans in diverse sectors, to launching a podcast during the curfew due to the Covid-19 pandemic. I have also worked as a journalist and content creator and have collaborated closely with CSR programs and HR departments. All this has given me a solid understanding of corporate communications and media dynamics.
I enjoy understanding an organization's business, key market drivers and communication needs as much as devising strategic plans to help it to achieve its objectives and increase audience and stakeholder engagement. Aware of the importance of continuous education to improve skills, especially in rapidly changing environments, I am currently expanding in the web development field (I have created this website myself!).
I believe in the value of curiosity, taking a fresh look at things, constantly wondering and trying to find answers to everyday challenges. That's why you can find me hiking a trail, doing pilates, learning modern calligraphy strokes, trying to make bread without kneading it, leading a short story club or going scuba diving.
Likewise, I firmly believe that the core value of communication lies in creating strong connections among people, communities, users, audiences, companies or clients to bring wills together and achieve common goals.
